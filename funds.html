How We Use Funds

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to cleaning up our planet and promoting sustainable living. We rely on the generosity of donors and supporters to fund our initiatives and make a positive impact on the environment.

  • Clean-up Events: Funds are used to organize and execute clean-up events in local communities, parks, beaches, and other public spaces. This includes purchasing supplies such as trash bags, gloves, and tools.
  • Educational Programs: We develop educational materials and programs to raise awareness about environmental issues and promote sustainable practices. Funds help us create and distribute these resources.
  • Community Outreach: We engage with local communities to encourage participation in our initiatives. Funds are used for outreach efforts, including marketing materials and community events.
  • Operational Costs: As a non-profit organization, we have operational costs such as website maintenance, administrative expenses, and printing materials. Funds help us cover these essential costs.

We use secure payment processing to manage our funds safely. Our payment partner is Collectiv. They accept various payment methods and ensure all transactions are processed securely.
